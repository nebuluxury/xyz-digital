The fine print (there isn't much)

Common questions

Is it really $500 with no catch?

Yes. $500 one-time for up to five custom pages. No hidden fees and no mandatory monthly plan. Hosting and your domain are the only separate costs, and they're inexpensive. We'll set everything up with you.

How is it so affordable?

We pair 45 years of senior experience with an AI-accelerated workflow, so we work about 5× faster than a traditional agency. We pass that speed on to you as a lower price, not a lower-quality result.

How fast will my site be ready?

Most $500 sites are done in a matter of days once we have your content and branding, rather than the weeks or months agencies usually take. You'll get a clear timeline on the consult.

What do you need from me to start?

Your logo (or we can make one), any photos you'd like used, your services and hours, and a sense of the look you want. Don't have it all polished? We'll help pull it together, including writing your copy.

Do you write the content and copy?

Yes. We'll write clear, persuasive copy for every page and refine it with you. If you already have text, we'll happily use and polish it.

What about photos and images?

We use professional stock and AI-generated imagery at no extra cost, and we offer product and business photography as an add-on if you want original shots.

How does hosting work?

Two easy options: we set the site up on your existing hosting for free, or we host and manage it for you for $29/mo (fast servers, SSL, backups, and uptime all handled).

What does support include?

Ongoing help and edits. Pay as you go, buy prepaid credits, or get unlimited chat support for $29/mo. Bundle hosting and unlimited support together for $59/mo.

Do I own the website?

Completely. The site is yours to keep, host anywhere, and edit. We can also maintain it for you if you'd prefer to never touch it.

Can I update the site myself?

Yes. We can build it so you can make edits, or you can leave changes to us on a support plan. Your call.

Will it work on phones?

Always. Every site we build is fully responsive and looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Is it good for Google / SEO?

Yes. Every site ships with SEO foundations built in (fast load times, clean structure, and proper metadata). We also offer full SEO and SEM campaigns run by a 25-year CMO.

What if I need more than 5 pages or e-commerce?

No problem. We'll quote the extra scope transparently. Many clients start with the $500 site and grow into a larger build, a store, or a web app.

Can you also do marketing and AI?

Yes. We're a full studio: SEO/SEM, an AI chatbot, a voice agent that answers your phones, or a full automation setup that connects all your business tools. It all lives under one roof.

Do you require a long contract?

No. The website is a one-time project, and monthly plans (hosting and support) can be cancelled anytime. No lock-in.

What industries do you work with?

All of them. We've built for auto shops, dry cleaners, dentists, restaurants, nonprofits, e-commerce, and more. If you serve customers, we can help.
